The Tech Job Hiring Landscape in 2020

August 22, 2020


As a recent graduate of what some refer to as a coding bootcamp (I'm leaving comments on the Lambda School experience for another post), I have a vested interest in understanding the current state of hiring affairs. If you've stumbled across this blog post from the future, I would confidently assume your familiarity with COVID-19 and its impact on everything from mundane errands to the most salient political matters. With the restrictions imposed by the virus on social proximity, hiring for jobs that would normally require an in-person interview saw a dramatic change almost overnight. When I began my foray into Lambda School's Data Science program, I was advised to set my sights away from remote employment opportunities; with the advent of COVID-19, remote work has become the norm, albeit begrudgingly. With this impact in mind, I wanted to investigate the current state of hiring in the tech sector. In this blog post, I resorted to Indeed for insights on the tech hiring status.
