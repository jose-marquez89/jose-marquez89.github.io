Readrr - A better way to track your books!

Readrr is an effort to take the most helpful aspects of book tracking services and blend them with those that could use some improvement. Recommendations are one such aspect in need of some help; they can often be unhelpful and in some cases, plain wrong. As part of the data science team, I am working on improving these recommendations using a blend of collaborative and content-based filtering, focusing largely on providing useful suggestions with less initial data.

A natural language processing application powered by Basilica, a word2vec API. Enter some proposed tweet text and the web-based application will analyze it and decide which of the two chosen accounts may have originated the tweet.
